Sentinel privacy policy

How Sentinel handles SMS-related data

This privacy policy describes how Sentinel collects, uses, and protects information associated with the Sentinel SMS program.

Information collected

  • Mobile phone number provided by the user.
  • Consent status and messaging preferences.
  • Message delivery metadata needed to operate the service.
  • User-requested reminder or conversational context necessary to deliver messages.

How information is used

  • To send the reminders, assistant follow-ups, and updates the user requested.
  • To honor opt-out, support, and compliance obligations.
  • To maintain service security, reliability, and auditability.

Sharing policy

Sentinel does not sell, rent, or share SMS opt-in data with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to deliver the messaging service, comply with law, or protect the service from misuse.

User choices

  • Reply STOP to unsubscribe from SMS messages at any time.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Users may decline to opt in and can revoke consent later.

Data retention and security

Sentinel retains only the information needed to operate the messaging program, maintain basic records of consent, and satisfy legal or operational requirements. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect stored information.

Program links

Additional SMS program details are available on the program overview and in the Terms and Conditions.

For help with the SMS program, reply HELP to a Sentinel message.
